Concept-Based Teaching

The school emphasizes deep understanding rather than rote memorization. Concept-based teaching ensures that students grasp the “why” and “how” behind every topic. This approach strengthens analytical thinking and long-term retention. .

Activity-Based Learning

Learning at Park View Academy is dynamic and experiential. Activity-based teaching methods include hands-on exercises, group tasks, projects, demonstrations, and interactive sessions.

Continuous Assessment System

The school follows a structured and continuous evaluation system to monitor student progress. Instead of relying solely on final examinations, students are assessed through periodic tests, assignments, projects, and classroom performance

AI-Based Learning Approach

Park View Academy incorporates advanced AI-supported learning techniques to enhance the educational experience. This approach enables personalized learning pathways, helping students learn at their own pace and level of understanding.
